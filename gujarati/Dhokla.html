Gujarati Dhokla

Recipe:

Ingredients

Instructions
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp eno fruit salt
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, ginger-green chili paste, turmeric powder, salt, and oil to make a smooth batter.
  • Let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes.
  • Grease a dhokla steamer or a plate that fits into a steamer.
  • Add eno fruit salt to the batter and mix well. The batter will become frothy.
  • Immediately pour the batter into the greased plate and steam for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  • For tempering, heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida, curry leaves, and green chilies. Saute for a minute.
  • Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla.
  • Cut into squares, garnish with fresh coriander leaves, and serve.
